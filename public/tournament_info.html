Tournament Format

  • Each team will consist of five players, ranked in order from #1 to #5.
  • In the Preliminary Rounds, schools will face-off against each other in a round-robin format, with the #1 ranked players playing each other, the #2 ranked players playing each other, and so forth.
  • Players earn 3 points for a win, 1 point for a draw and 0 points for a loss.
  • Timing Controls: 8|2
  • Following the preliminary rounds, the two highest scoring teams will face-off in a final match.
  • The winner of the final match will receive the team award.
  • The top 3 highest scoring individual players will receive awards.
